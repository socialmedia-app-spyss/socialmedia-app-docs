Shri Pathanjali Yoga Shikshana Samiti (SPYSS)

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Shri Pathanjali Yoga Shikshana Samiti (SPYSS) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the SPYSS mobile application handles information when you use the application.

2. Information We Collect

The SPYSS App does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Address
  • Payment Information

The application is designed to provide information about SPYSS branches, events, and activities without requiring users to create an account.

3. Location Services

The SPYSS App may request access to your device location, including precise location when permitted by you, to:

  • Display nearby SPYSS branches
  • Calculate accurate distance to nearby branches
  • Assist with navigation and directions

Location information is used only while providing these features and is not stored or retained by SPYSS. You may choose approximate location where supported by your device, but distance-based results may be less accurate.

Users may deny or revoke location permission at any time through their device settings.

4. Application Content and Backend Services

The SPYSS App retrieves publicly available branch information, event details, notifications, and other application content from backend services managed by SPYSS using Supabase.

These services are used solely for delivering application content and do not require users to provide personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

The application may utilize third-party services including:

  • Supabase (backend content delivery)
  • Google Maps Platform
  • Google Play Services

These services may process technical information necessary for their operation in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

SPYSS does not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information because the application does not collect personal information from users.

7. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the systems and services used to deliver application content.

8. Children's Privacy

The SPYSS App is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the “Last Updated” date and publishing the updated policy within the application.
